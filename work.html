Cross - Platform Ground Control and Beyond LOS Missions

This quadrotor can be flown using a radio, a Mac, a PC, a mobile phone . . . over wifi, LTE, sms, VSAT . . . using WhatsApp, Telegram and Messenger for retrieving data and logs. Our light weight comm. protocols and APIs make it possible to establish various standard communication links in a plug and play fashion. This extends operational range and widens the recipient data - link base. As an example, our drones can be operated from a base in Rajasthan by an operator in Punjab while critical imagery can be directly received at New Delhi, all within a latency of under a second.

Multirole Modularity for Extended Missions and Advanced Aeronautics

Modular design and assembly of our aircrafts lets us extend our flight envelope by convening different combinations of aerodynamic surfaces, engines, structures and other design variables. To say, we can transform our aircraft from a heavy lifter cargo plane to an endurance optimized reconnaisance UAV within an hour.
The best of aerospace engineering and machine assembly goes into optimization and fabrication of our modular parts and out comes an advanced high - performance aircraft which can accomplish custom mission - profiles.
We have extended this degree of customization even further to multirotors.

Drones for Developers, Swarms and More . . .

Our Linux based flight controller with integrated microprocessor allows a developer to program higher level flight commands in Python and C++ using our inhouse communication library. This allows an execution of a plethora of innovative ideas using our UAVs as aerial platforms. Programming a swarm of 10 multirotors to build a Burma Bridge in less than an hour is one of the many possibilities that we have explored. Similarly, you can program our drone to help with your ping - pong practice. AI based programs can also be run on our UAVs equipped with on - board GPU(s).
